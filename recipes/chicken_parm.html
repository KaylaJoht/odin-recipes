Parmesan Chicken

Description

This Italian-American classic is a classic for a reason! Relatively simple, but extrordinarily delicious. While you can enjoy this recipe as just plain chicken, I decided to help elevate it by introducing some noodles, and broccoli. Those additional meals elevate the flavor to something even more delicious.

Just bread your chicken in a mix of breadcrumbs and parmesan, bake in the oven for a few minutes to melt a topping of mozzerella, and you'll be left with possibly the best way to eat chicken, bursting with incredible amounts of flavor. With that in mind, lets get started!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of Breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Paprika
  • 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese
  • 24oz Marinara sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Optional

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Broccoli
  • Lemon juice
  • Chili flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees
  2. In a small pot, heat the marinara sauce. Once it boils, leave it on low heat
  3. Put your flour on a plate, beat the eggs on a separate plate
  4. Mix the breadcrumbs, parmesan, salt, pepper, and paprika on a plate
  5. Take your chicken and slice it in half across the side. The idea is to get two slices of chicken at half the thickness. Similar to butterfly-ing chicken
  6. Dredge the chicken in the flour mixture first, rememebr to shake off excess flour. Then the egg wash, and finally thr breadcrumb mixture.
  7. Pour oil into a pan and heat on Medium heat
  8. Cook the chicken for about 4 minutes on each side
  9. When the chicken is done, move it to a baking dish
  10. Put a dollop of marinara on top, sprinkle it with mozzerella cheese, and top with salt and pepper. Place in the oven for about 10 minutes, or until the cheese melts
  11. Remove from oven and enjoy!

Optional

If you wish to make the side dishes, here you go

  1. You can start this any time, but I would start it after instruction 2
  2. In a large pot boil some water. Add salt to taste
  3. when the water boils, add the spaghetti noodles. Cook until al dente
  4. Strain the noodles, while in the strainer, add olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix
  5. Add a small amount of Marinara sauce
  6. they are ready to serve
  7. While the chicken is in the oven, either re-use the chicken pan, or get a clean pan
  8. Heat to a medium heat. When hot, add lemon juice, broccoli, and chili flakes
  9. Cook for a few minutes until the broccol is cooked to your liking. You might need to add a a lid to stream it a little
  10. With the chicken done, plate with spaghetti and brocolli
  11. Enjoy!
