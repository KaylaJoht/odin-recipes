Parmesan Chicken

Description

This Italian-American classic is a classic for a reason! Relatively simple, but extrordinarily delicious. While you can enjoy this recipe as just plain chicken, I decided to help elevate it by introducing some noodles, and broccoli. Those additional meals elevate the flavor to something even more delicious.

Just bread your chicken in a mix of breadcrumbs and parmesan, bake in the oven for a few minutes to melt a topping of mozzerella, and you'll be left with possibly the best way to eat chicken, bursting with incredible amounts of flavor. With that in mind, lets get started!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Chicken breasts
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of Breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Paprika
  • 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese
  • 24oz Marinara sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Optional

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Broccoli
  • Lemon juice
  • Chili flakes
